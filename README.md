## XK0-006 Exam Information

**Number of questions:** Maximum of 90 (multiple-choice and performance-based)
**Duration:** 90 minutes
**Passing score:** 720 (on a scale of 100–900)
**Language:** English
**Recommended experience:** 12 months of hands-on experience with Linux servers; CompTIA A+, Network+, or Server+, or comparable knowledge is recommended.

The exam measures proficiency in managing, configuring, and troubleshooting Linux systems, including fundamental command-line operations, permissions, system services, and networking.

## Key Objectives Analysis

Key focus domains of the Linux+ exam include:

**System Management:** Installation, configuration, and maintenance of Linux operating systems.
**Security:** Implementing secure authentication, firewalls, and access controls.
**Scripting and Automation:** Utilizing Bash scripts to automate administrative tasks.
**Networking:** Configuring network interfaces and troubleshooting connectivity issues.
**Troubleshooting:** Identifying and resolving common system and application issues.
